Introduction

Last updated: March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how BuddyGo uses them across the BuddyGo mobile application, website, and related services.

It describes the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

This Cookie Policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy, which explains how we collect and process personal data.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (such as a smartphone, tablet, or computer) when you use a website or mobile application.

These files allow websites and applications to recognize your device and remember certain information about your activity.

In addition to cookies, BuddyGo may use similar technologies such as:

  • device identifiers
  • SDKs (Software Development Kits) integrated into mobile applications
  • analytics tools
  • technical event tracking systems

These technologies help us understand how the platform is used and ensure that the service works correctly.

Cookies can be:

  • Session cookies, which are automatically deleted when you close your browser or end a session.
  • Persistent cookies, which remain stored on your device for a limited period of time.

Some cookies are placed directly by BuddyGo, while others may be placed by trusted third-party service providers.

Why We Use Cookies

BuddyGo uses cookies and similar technologies for several purposes related to the functioning, security, and improvement of our services.

Which cookies we use

Essential cookies

Based on the performance of our services, we use essential cookies to:

  • Record and process technical events and user interactions in order to provide the core functionalities of the BuddyGo platform, including account access, messaging, profile discovery, and matching features.
  • Support the development, testing, and improvement of new features within the application.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines, including enabling moderation actions, detecting suspicious activity, and preventing abuse of the platform.

Based on our legitimate interests, we use essential cookies to:

  • Ensure the proper functioning, stability, and security of the BuddyGo services, including detecting technical issues such as crashes, bugs, or service interruptions.
  • Protect the BuddyGo community by helping identify suspicious behavior, fraud attempts, or other safety risks on the platform.
  • Improve the overall performance and reliability of our services.

To comply with our legal obligations, we may use essential cookies to:

  • Generate statistics and technical information required to meet transparency, accountability, and online safety obligations.

Essential cookies are necessary for the operation of the BuddyGo services and therefore do not require the user's prior consent.

Analytics cookies

BuddyGo may use analytics cookies, where permitted by applicable laws, to better understand how users interact with our services.

These cookies help us measure the performance of the BuddyGo app and website, generate usage statistics, and improve the user experience.

Analytics tools may also help us understand how users discover the BuddyGo app, allowing us to improve marketing campaigns and avoid targeting users who have already installed the app.

Users may be able to manage or withdraw their consent to analytics tracking through their device privacy settings or through privacy settings available within the BuddyGo application.

For iOS users, tracking preferences may also be controlled through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework available in device settings.

Third-party integrations

In some cases, BuddyGo may integrate features provided by third-party services within the app or website.

For example, our website may allow users to view embedded content such as videos hosted on external platforms. When interacting with such features, certain data may be processed by the third-party provider according to their own privacy policies.

BuddyGo does not control how these third-party services process personal data.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Depending on your device and location, you may be able to manage cookie and tracking preferences directly through:

  • your device settings
  • your browser settings
  • privacy settings available within the BuddyGo application

For example, iOS users can manage tracking permissions through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) settings.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

BuddyGo may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our services.

When significant changes occur, we may notify users through the application or update the date at the top of this page.
