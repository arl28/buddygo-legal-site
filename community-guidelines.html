Community Guidelines

These guidelines explain how to use BuddyGo respectfully and safely, and what behavior is not allowed on the platform.

Overview

BuddyGo is a social travel platform designed to help people discover new places and meet other travelers around the world. Our goal is to create a community where users can safely connect, share experiences, and explore destinations together.

To maintain a positive environment for everyone, all users must follow these Community Guidelines when using the BuddyGo application or related services. These guidelines explain what behavior is acceptable on the platform and what types of conduct are not allowed. By using BuddyGo, you agree to respect these rules as well as our Terms of Service.

Safety First

User safety is a core priority at BuddyGo. We want the platform to be a place where travelers can connect and interact without fear of harassment, scams, or harmful behavior.

BuddyGo does not tolerate violence, threats, harassment, hate speech, fraud, or any activity that may put users at risk. If you encounter suspicious behavior, a fake profile, or a user asking for money or engaging in inappropriate conduct, you should report it immediately through the reporting tools available in the app. Our moderation team reviews reports and takes action when necessary to protect the community.

Authentic Profiles

BuddyGo works best when users are genuine and transparent. For this reason, users must provide accurate information when creating their profiles.

Profiles should represent the real person using the account. This includes using your real age and profile photos that clearly represent you. Creating fake profiles, impersonating another person, or providing misleading identity information is not permitted. Accounts that violate these rules may be suspended or permanently removed from the platform.

Respect for Others

BuddyGo is built around respectful interactions between travelers. All users are expected to treat each other with kindness and consideration.

Behavior intended to intimidate, insult, harass, or harm another person is not allowed. This includes bullying, discrimination, hate speech, unwanted sexual advances, or sharing private information about someone else without their consent. If another user makes you feel uncomfortable or unsafe, you can block and report them directly through the app.

Privacy and Personal Information

Protecting personal privacy is extremely important when interacting online. While BuddyGo encourages users to connect and share travel experiences, users should always remain cautious when sharing personal information.

Sensitive information such as home addresses, financial details, identification documents, or other private data should never be shared publicly on the platform. Users must also respect the privacy of others and may not publish or distribute another person's personal information without their consent.

Platform Integrity

BuddyGo is intended for genuine social connections between travelers and not for commercial exploitation. Any activity designed to deceive or exploit other users is strictly prohibited.

This includes scams, fraudulent activity, impersonation, spam, or attempts to promote or sell products and services without authorization. Users who engage in deceptive practices or attempt to manipulate other members of the community may have their accounts permanently banned.

Content and Behavior Standards

BuddyGo encourages users to express themselves, but certain types of content are not permitted on the platform.

Content that is sexually explicit, violent, graphic, illegal, or intended to shock or disturb others is not allowed. Any material that promotes illegal activity or harms the safety and well-being of other users may be removed and may result in account suspension or termination.

Meeting Other Travelers

BuddyGo helps travelers connect, and some users may choose to meet in person. However, all offline interactions are the responsibility of the individuals involved.

Users should always exercise caution when meeting someone they met online. We recommend meeting in public places, informing friends or family of your plans, and using good judgment when interacting with others. BuddyGo cannot monitor or control interactions that take place outside the platform.

Protection of Minors

BuddyGo is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. Any activity involving minors, including attempts to contact or exploit minors, is strictly prohibited.

Accounts found engaging in such activity will be permanently banned and may be reported to the appropriate authorities when required by law.

Enforcement

All users must respect these Community Guidelines. When violations occur, BuddyGo may take appropriate action depending on the severity of the situation.

These actions may include removing content, issuing warnings, temporarily suspending accounts, or permanently banning users from the platform. In cases involving illegal activity or serious safety risks, BuddyGo may cooperate with law enforcement authorities when required.

Reporting Violations

Users play an important role in maintaining a safe community. If you encounter behavior or content that violates these guidelines, you should report it through the reporting tools available within the BuddyGo application.

Our moderation team reviews reports and takes appropriate action as quickly as possible. Abuse of the reporting system, including false or malicious reports, may result in disciplinary action.
