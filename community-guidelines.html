Community Guidelines

BuddyGo is a travel and connection space. These rules keep people safe and conversations healthy.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

What we encourage

  • Respect, kindness, and clear consent.
  • Authentic profiles and honest information.
  • Quick reporting of risky behavior.

What is not allowed

  • Harassment, threats, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • Unsolicited explicit sexual content.
  • Scams, impersonation, or fraud.
  • Sharing someone else’s personal data.

Moderation

We apply zero tolerance for serious abuse. Accounts may be suspended or removed.

  • Human and automated reviews.
  • Report history is considered.
  • Appeals via support@buddygo.app.

Tips for a trustworthy profile

  • Add clear and recent photos.
  • Explain your travel intentions.
  • Stay professional in messages.
