Delete Account

You can request the permanent deletion of your BuddyGo account and associated personal data at any time. BuddyGo provides users with the ability to delete their account directly from within the mobile application.

Deleting your account will permanently remove your access to the BuddyGo platform and your profile will no longer be visible to other users.

Please note that account deletion is irreversible.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your BuddyGo account directly from the application by following these steps.

Open the BuddyGo application and go to your account settings. Inside the settings menu, navigate to the account section and select the option to delete your account. You will be asked to confirm your request before the deletion process begins.

Once the request is confirmed, your account will be scheduled for permanent deletion.

What Happens After Deletion

When your BuddyGo account is deleted, your profile will no longer be accessible and other users will no longer be able to view or interact with your account.

Access to the BuddyGo services will be permanently removed. Your profile information and associated personal data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period of time.

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal obligations, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our Terms of Service.

Important Notice

Deleting the BuddyGo application from your device does not automatically delete your BuddyGo account.

To permanently delete your account, you must complete the deletion process through the account settings inside the BuddyGo app.

Need Help

If you cannot access your account or need assistance with the account deletion process, you can contact our support team.

Email: support@buddygo.app

Please include any relevant details that may help us process your request more efficiently.
