Account & data deletion

You can delete your BuddyGo account at any time. Here is the process and what happens to your data.

Standard processing time: within 7 days

From the app

  • Open the BuddyGo app.
  • Go to Settings > Account.
  • Select “Delete my account”.
  • Confirm the deletion.

By email

If you can’t access the app, email support@buddygo.app with:

  • The email associated with the account.
  • Your phone number (if used).
  • A clear request for deletion.

What happens next?

  • Your profile becomes invisible immediately.
  • Messages and content are removed on our purge cycles.
  • Minimal legal data may be retained for compliance.

You can also request full erasure of your personal data.
