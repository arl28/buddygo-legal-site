Safety Center

Our Commitment to Safety

At BuddyGo, the safety of our community is a top priority. Our goal is to create a space where travelers can connect, share experiences, and meet new people in a respectful and secure environment.

We are continuously working to improve our tools, policies, and moderation systems to protect users and reduce harmful behavior on the platform.

While BuddyGo provides safety features and moderation systems, users should always remain cautious when interacting with others online or when meeting someone in person.

Safety is shared. We build the tools, and you control your choices.

Safety Tools in the App

BuddyGo provides several built-in tools that allow users to protect themselves and control their interactions on the platform.

Users can block other members if they feel uncomfortable or wish to stop communication. Blocking prevents that user from contacting you again or viewing your profile.

Users can also report suspicious behavior, inappropriate content, or violations of our Community Guidelines directly within the application. Our moderation team reviews reports and takes action when necessary.

These tools help us maintain a safe and respectful community.

Reporting and Moderation

BuddyGo is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for everyone in our community. If you encounter behavior, content, or an account that violates our Community Guidelines or Terms of Service, we encourage you to report it directly through the reporting tools available in the BuddyGo application.

Users can report profiles, messages, or inappropriate behavior directly within the app. Once a report is submitted, our moderation team reviews the information carefully in order to assess the situation and determine whether the reported content or account violates our policies.

When submitting a report, users may be asked to select a reason for the report and optionally provide additional information or context. Providing accurate details helps our moderation team review the situation more effectively.

Depending on the severity of the violation, BuddyGo may take different actions. These actions may include issuing a warning to the user, removing content, restricting certain features, temporarily suspending the account, or permanently removing the account from the platform.

Reports are handled confidentially. The user being reported will not be notified of the identity of the person who submitted the report.

In situations involving illegal activity, threats to personal safety, or other serious violations, BuddyGo may cooperate with law enforcement or relevant authorities when required by applicable laws.

Our moderation systems combine human review, internal tools, and safety policies designed to protect our community and maintain a respectful platform for travelers.

Protecting Your Privacy

BuddyGo encourages all users to be mindful when sharing personal information online. Protecting your privacy is an important part of staying safe when interacting with others through the platform.

We strongly recommend avoiding the sharing of sensitive personal information such as your home address, financial information, identification documents, travel documents, or passwords with people you have only recently met online.

Even when conversations feel friendly or trustworthy, remember that you are interacting with people you may not know personally. Always take time to build trust before sharing personal details.

Users should also respect the privacy of others. Sharing someone else's personal information, photos, or private details without their consent is strictly prohibited and may lead to moderation action.

For more information about how BuddyGo handles personal data and protects user privacy, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Meeting Safely in Real Life

BuddyGo helps travelers connect with other people nearby, and some users may choose to meet in person after connecting through the platform.

If you decide to meet someone you met on BuddyGo, always prioritize your personal safety.

We recommend meeting in public places such as cafes, restaurants, or other busy locations. Inform a trusted friend or family member about your plans and consider sharing your location with them during the meeting.

Avoid meeting in isolated locations or private accommodations for the first time. It is also advisable to arrange your own transportation and remain aware of your surroundings.

While BuddyGo provides a platform to help people connect, we cannot control interactions that occur outside the application. Users should always exercise caution and good judgment when meeting someone in person.

Preventing Scams and Fraud

BuddyGo maintains a strict policy against scams, fraud, and any attempt to exploit other users.

Be cautious of anyone asking for money, financial assistance, gift cards, or payment through external services. Requests for financial support from someone you have only met online are often a warning sign of fraudulent activity.

Users should never send money, share banking information, or provide sensitive financial details to individuals they have met through the platform.

If you encounter suspicious behavior, financial requests, or accounts that appear to be impersonating someone else, please report the account immediately using the reporting tools available in the BuddyGo application.

Our moderation team investigates reports carefully and may remove accounts that violate our policies or engage in fraudulent activities.

Protection of Minors

BuddyGo is intended exclusively for users aged 18 and older.

We do not allow minors to create accounts or use the platform. Any activity involving attempts to contact, exploit, or harm minors is strictly prohibited.

Accounts involved in such behavior will be permanently banned and may be reported to the appropriate authorities where required by applicable laws.

BuddyGo is committed to maintaining a responsible and secure platform that prevents exploitation and protects vulnerable individuals.

Our Safety Approach

BuddyGo combines technology, human moderation, and community standards to maintain a safer environment for users.

Our safety approach includes built-in reporting and blocking tools that allow users to quickly report inappropriate behavior or block accounts that make them uncomfortable. Our moderation systems review reports and take action when violations of our policies are identified.

We also rely on clear community guidelines and platform rules to define acceptable behavior and ensure that users interact respectfully with one another.

However, online safety is a shared responsibility. While BuddyGo works continuously to improve its safety systems, users are encouraged to remain cautious, respectful, and responsible in all interactions on and off the platform.

Need Help?

If you encounter suspicious behavior, harassment, scams, or any safety concerns while using BuddyGo, please report it directly in the application.
