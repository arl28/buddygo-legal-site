Support

Need help?

If you need help with BuddyGo, our support team is here to assist you. Whether you are experiencing a technical issue, need to report a user, or have questions about your account, we will do our best to respond as quickly as possible.

Our typical response time is 24 to 48 business hours.

Contact

If you need assistance, you can contact the BuddyGo support team using the following email address:

  • General support: support@buddygo.app

Please include as much detail as possible in your message so our team can assist you efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

I did not receive my login code

If you did not receive your login verification code, please check your spam or junk email folder. If the problem persists, try requesting a new code from the login screen. If you still cannot access your account, contact our support team for assistance.

How does BuddyGo work?

BuddyGo is a social travel platform designed to help travelers connect with people nearby or in destinations they are visiting. Users can discover profiles, match with other travelers, chat, and explore places together.

The app focuses on meeting like-minded travelers and sharing experiences safely.

How do subscriptions work?

BuddyGo offers two subscription options that enhance the user experience.

The BuddyGo explorer + subscription provides additional features inside the app such as seeing who liked your profile, accessing personality quizzes, and improving your visibility within the platform.

The BuddyGo infinity subscription includes all features from BuddyGo explorer + and also gives access to exclusive travel advantages. Premium members may benefit from special partnerships with hotels or travel partners. These advantages can be accessed through a QR code generated in the app, which can be presented at participating hotels or partners when checking out.

Subscription details and prices are always displayed clearly inside the application before purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the platform where the purchase was made.

If you subscribed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your device's subscription settings. Once canceled, the subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period and will not renew automatically.

Where are payments processed?

All subscription payments are processed securely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. BuddyGo does not store or have access to your payment card information.

For billing issues or refund requests, you may need to contact the platform where the purchase was made.

I want to report a user

If you encounter inappropriate behavior or a user that violates our Community Guidelines, you can report the account directly inside the BuddyGo application using the report feature. Our moderation team reviews all reports to help maintain a safe community.

Can I block another user?

Yes. If another user makes you uncomfortable, you can block them directly from their profile or conversation. Blocking a user prevents them from contacting you again.

Why was my account suspended?

Accounts may be suspended if they violate BuddyGo’s Terms of Service or Community Guidelines.

This may include behavior such as harassment, scams, fake profiles, or inappropriate content. If you believe your account was suspended by mistake, you can contact our support team for assistance.

I want to delete my account

You can delete your BuddyGo account at any time from the application settings. Once your account is deleted, your profile will no longer be visible and your personal data will be removed or anonymized according to our Privacy Policy.

For more information, please refer to our Account Deletion page.

I found a bug or technical issue

If you experience a bug or technical issue while using BuddyGo, please contact our support team and include the following information:

  • Your device model
  • Your operating system version
  • A description of the issue
  • Screenshots if possible

Providing these details helps our team investigate and resolve the issue more quickly.

Is BuddyGo safe?

BuddyGo uses moderation tools and reporting systems to help maintain a respectful and safe environment. Users can block and report suspicious or inappropriate behavior directly within the app.

However, users should always exercise caution when interacting with people online or meeting someone in person.

Can I change my email address?

Yes. You can update your email address from the account settings in the BuddyGo application. If you experience any issues updating your email, please contact our support team.

How does the personality quiz work?

The BuddyGo personality quiz is a short experience designed to help travelers discover compatible people on the platform.

The quiz consists of 5 quick questions about your travel style, preferences, and personality. Once you complete the quiz, BuddyGo will automatically generate potential matches based on your answers.

At the end of the quiz, you will be shown four suggested traveler profiles that match your personality. These profiles will initially appear blurred, creating a surprise match experience.

The personality quiz is available with BuddyGo Explorer+ and BuddyGo Infinity subscriptions.

Can I use BuddyGo while traveling?

Yes. BuddyGo is designed specifically for travelers. You can use the app to discover people near your current location or in destinations you plan to visit. This makes it easier to connect with other travelers and locals when exploring new places.

What should I do if someone asks me for money?

BuddyGo strictly prohibits scams or financial requests between users.

If someone asks you for money, gifts, cryptocurrency, or financial assistance, you should stop the conversation immediately and report the user through the app. Never send money to someone you only know through an online platform.

Our moderation team reviews these reports and may suspend or permanently ban accounts involved in fraudulent activity.

How do I stay safe when meeting someone from BuddyGo?

If you decide to meet someone you connected with on BuddyGo, always prioritize your safety.

We recommend meeting in public places such as cafés, restaurants, or popular public areas. Inform a friend or family member about your plans and avoid sharing sensitive personal information with people you have just met.

BuddyGo cannot monitor offline interactions, so users should always exercise caution and good judgment when meeting others.

I have a question about my personal data

If you would like to access, update, or delete your personal data, you can contact our privacy team at: privacy@buddygo.app.

For more details about how we handle personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
