Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data BuddyGo collects, why, and how you stay in control.

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Data we collect

  • Profile info: name, photo, bio, preferences.
  • Account data: email, phone number, login identifiers.
  • Usage data: interactions, messages, reports.
  • Technical data: device, IP, diagnostic logs.
  • Location: only with your permission to suggest relevant matches.

Why we use your data

  • Create and secure your account.
  • Power matching, chat, and travel features.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and support moderation.
  • Improve the app and user experience.

Your choices and rights

  • Access and correction: update info in the app.
  • Deletion: delete your account or request data erasure.
  • Export: request a copy of your personal data.
  • Consent: withdraw permissions (e.g., location).

For any request, contact support@buddygo.app.

Data sharing

We never sell your data. It may be shared with:

  • Essential service providers (hosting, analytics, support).
  • Authorities when required by law.
  • Safety partners to detect and prevent abuse.

Retention and security

  • Data is kept as long as needed to provide the service.
  • We use technical and organizational safeguards.
  • Deleted conversations are removed from servers on our purge cycles.
