Introduction

Last updated: March 2026

BuddyGo is a social travel and discovery application designed to help people explore destinations, meet new travel companions, and create meaningful connections through shared experiences.

Our mission is to make travel more social, accessible, and safe by connecting travelers with like-minded people nearby. BuddyGo allows users to discover profiles, match with other travelers, chat, and explore cities together.

In addition to social discovery features, BuddyGo may also provide travel-related advantages through partnerships with hotels and travel partners. Certain premium members may gain access to exclusive offers, perks, or discounts designed to enhance their travel experience.

This Privacy Policy explains how BuddyGo collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services.

In this Privacy Policy, you will find detailed information about how we process personal data, what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we ensure its protection. We encourage you to read this policy carefully, as it applies whenever you use the BuddyGo application, our website, or any related services.

We have written this Privacy Policy to be as clear and transparent as possible. If you have any questions about this policy or about how your personal data is used, you can contact our team at:

support@buddygo.app

Notice at Collection of Personal Information

We collect certain personal information described in the section “Data We Collect” for the purposes explained in the section “How We Use Your Data”.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain the security and integrity of the BuddyGo platform.

Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all products and services operated by BuddyGo, including the BuddyGo mobile application, our website, and any related features or services.

Definition of Personal Data

By “personal information” or “personal data”, we mean any information that can identify you directly or indirectly.

This may include information such as your name, profile photo, email address, location data, device information, or identifiers associated with your account.

When you use the BuddyGo application or visit our website, we may collect certain information about you in order to operate the service, improve the user experience, and ensure the safety of our community.

Who We Are

BuddyGo is a travel and social discovery mobile application designed to help people meet other travelers, explore destinations, and create meaningful connections through shared experiences.

The BuddyGo platform is operated by BuddyGo LLC, a company based in Florida, United States.

For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, BuddyGo LLC acts as the data controller of the personal data described in this Privacy Policy. This means we determine how and why your personal information is collected and processed when you use our services.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing of your personal data, you can contact us at:

support@buddygo.app

Data We Collect

3.1 Data You Provide to Us Directly

When you use BuddyGo, you may provide us with certain personal information directly in order to create and manage your account and use the features of our services.

Account Information

To use BuddyGo, you must create an account. During the sign-up process, we may ask you to provide basic personal information such as:

  • your email address
  • your username or display name
  • your date of birth or age
  • your profile photo
  • optional profile information such as a bio or travel interests

You may also choose to provide additional profile details to personalize your experience on the platform.

Some information, such as your email address or birth date, is used only for account management and security purposes and is not publicly visible to other users.

Social Login

BuddyGo may offer simplified sign-up options through third-party authentication providers such as Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In.

If you choose to use these services, we may receive certain information from those providers, such as:

  • your name
  • your email address
  • your Apple ID or Google account identifier

This information is used only to create and manage your BuddyGo account.

Profile Information

You may optionally provide additional information to complete your profile, such as:

  • profile description or bio
  • travel preferences
  • interests or activities
  • photos you upload

Some of this information may be visible to other users depending on your profile settings.

Your Interactions with Us

When you interact with our support team or use certain platform features, we may process information related to these interactions. For example:

  • contacting customer support
  • reporting another user or content
  • responding to surveys or feedback requests
  • submitting safety reports

This information is used to improve our services and maintain a safe community environment.

Location Information

BuddyGo may collect location information to improve the matching and discovery experience.

With your permission, we may collect:

  • Precise location data (GPS). This allows BuddyGo to show nearby users and help you discover people in the same city or destination.
  • Approximate location (IP address). If GPS access is not enabled, we may estimate your general location (such as city or region) based on your IP address.

You can manage location permissions through your device settings.

Content You Share

When using BuddyGo features, we process the content you share, such as:

  • profile photos
  • messages sent through the chat feature
  • travel-related content or posts

We also process technical information associated with this content, such as timestamps, device information, or IP address.

Please note that content you share with other users may be viewed by them and may potentially be saved or captured by their devices (for example, screenshots).

Camera, Photos and Media Access

To upload photos, send images, or use certain features, BuddyGo may request permission to access:

  • your camera
  • your photo library
  • media files stored on your device

You can control these permissions through your device settings.

3.2 Data We Collect Automatically

When you use BuddyGo, certain technical information is automatically collected in order to operate the service and improve the user experience.

Usage Information

We may collect information about how you use BuddyGo, including:

  • the date your account was created
  • the date of your last login
  • your account identifier
  • your interactions with features and other users
  • your app settings and preferences

This information helps us improve the platform and provide relevant features.

Device and Technical Information

We may collect information about the device you use to access BuddyGo, including:

  • IP address
  • device model and brand
  • operating system and version
  • app version
  • language settings
  • crash reports and diagnostics

This information helps us maintain the stability and security of the service.

Purchase and Subscription Information

If you subscribe to premium features or purchase services within BuddyGo, we may receive limited information about those purchases, such as:

  • subscription status
  • purchase history

Payment information is processed securely by third-party providers such as Apple App Store or Google Play Store, and BuddyGo does not store your payment card details.

3.3 Data We Receive from Third Parties

In certain cases, we may receive information from third parties.

Other Users

Other users may provide information about you when they:

  • report a profile or content
  • mention you in interactions
  • submit safety reports

Service Providers

We may receive information from trusted service providers who help operate BuddyGo, such as:

  • analytics providers
  • cloud hosting providers
  • app store platforms

These partners help us maintain and improve our services.

Legal and Safety Authorities

In certain situations, we may receive information from law enforcement authorities or safety organizations when required for legal compliance, safety investigations, or the protection of users.

How We Use Your Data

BuddyGo processes personal data in order to operate the platform, provide our services, improve the user experience, and maintain a safe environment for all users.

Below you can find the main purposes for which your personal data may be used.

4.1 To Manage Your Account and Provide Core Features

We use your personal data to create and manage your account and allow you to use the core features of the BuddyGo platform.

This includes:

  • creating and managing your user account
  • allowing you to access the BuddyGo application and its services
  • enabling interactions with other users, such as discovering profiles, matching, and messaging
  • managing profile information and user-generated content
  • responding to your questions, requests, or support inquiries

This processing is necessary for the performance of our services and the proper functioning of the BuddyGo platform.

4.2 To Improve Our Services and User Experience

We may use your data to understand how users interact with the platform and to improve the quality of our services.

This may include:

  • analyzing how users use the app and its features
  • improving the matching and discovery system
  • developing new features and improving existing ones
  • conducting surveys or collecting feedback
  • evaluating and improving the performance of the platform

This processing helps us better understand our community and continuously improve the BuddyGo experience.

4.3 To Enable Location-Based Discovery

BuddyGo may use location information in order to help users discover people nearby or in the same destination.

This may include:

  • suggesting nearby users based on location
  • improving travel discovery features
  • helping users connect with travelers in the same city or area

Location information is used only when permission is granted through your device settings.

4.4 To Manage Premium Features and Partnerships

BuddyGo may use certain information to manage premium features and services available to subscribers.

This may include:

  • managing subscriptions and premium memberships
  • enabling access to exclusive features
  • providing travel-related benefits such as offers or perks through partnerships with hotels or travel partners

Payment information is processed by third-party providers such as Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

4.5 To Maintain Platform Safety

We use certain information to help maintain a safe and respectful community.

This may include:

  • detecting and preventing fraudulent or suspicious activity
  • reviewing reports submitted by users
  • enforcing our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
  • moderating content when necessary

These measures help protect users and maintain trust within the BuddyGo community.

4.6 To Comply With Legal Obligations

In certain situations, we may process personal data in order to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

This may include:

  • responding to legal requests from authorities
  • complying with data protection regulations
  • handling requests related to user privacy rights
  • preserving information when legally required

We may also use personal information where necessary to protect our legal rights.

How Long We Keep Your Data

5.1 Account Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the BuddyGo services and maintain your account.

You may delete your account at any time through the account settings in the BuddyGo application. Once your account is deleted, your profile will no longer be visible to other users and you will no longer be able to access the BuddyGo services.

If an account remains inactive for an extended period of time, we may delete or anonymize the associated personal data in order to maintain the security and efficiency of the platform.

5.2 Specific Data Retention Periods

Different types of data may be stored for different periods depending on their purpose.

Examples include:

  • Messages and conversations - Messages exchanged between users may be stored for a limited period of time in order to ensure proper functioning of the chat service and to help investigate potential safety issues.
  • User-generated content - Profile information, photos, and other content remain stored while your account is active and are deleted or anonymized when your account is removed.
  • Support communications - Information related to requests sent to our support team may be kept for a reasonable period of time in order to resolve issues, improve our services, and defend our legal rights if necessary.
  • Location data - Location information used for discovery features is retained only for the time necessary to provide the service and is regularly updated or replaced by more recent location data.

5.3 Legal and Safety Retention

In certain circumstances, we may retain personal data for a longer period when necessary to:

  • comply with legal obligations
  • investigate potential violations of our Community Guidelines
  • resolve disputes
  • protect the safety of users
  • exercise or defend our legal rights

When data is no longer necessary for these purposes, it will be deleted or anonymized.

6.1 Data We Share

BuddyGo does not sell your personal data. However, we may share certain personal information in the situations described below.

BuddyGo Personnel

Authorized members of the BuddyGo team may access personal data when necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the platform.

Access to personal data is limited to what is necessary for each task and is subject to confidentiality obligations.

Service Providers

We may share certain personal data with trusted service providers who help us operate and improve BuddyGo.

These providers may include services that help us:

  • host and store application data
  • maintain the infrastructure and servers of the platform
  • analyze usage and performance of the app
  • provide cloud services
  • process customer support requests
  • manage subscriptions and in-app purchases

These providers are required to process personal data only according to our instructions and must comply with strict confidentiality and security obligations.

Business Partners

In some cases, BuddyGo may collaborate with trusted partners to provide travel-related services or benefits, such as hotel partnerships or travel offers.

In such situations, only the information necessary to provide the service may be shared, and only with your knowledge or consent when required.

Legal Requirements and Safety

We may disclose personal data when required by law or when we believe it is necessary to:

  • comply with legal obligations
  • respond to lawful requests from authorities
  • protect the safety of users
  • investigate illegal activities or violations of our policies
  • protect the rights, property, or security of BuddyGo or its users

Business Transfers

If BuddyGo is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred to the new entity as part of that transaction.

In such cases, this Privacy Policy will continue to apply to your personal data.

6.2 Data You Share With Other Users

When you use BuddyGo, you may share personal data directly with other users through the features of the platform.

For example, this may include:

  • your profile information
  • your photos
  • messages sent through the chat feature
  • other content you choose to share

Any information you share with other users may be visible to them. Please be mindful of the content you choose to share online.

BuddyGo cannot control how other users may use or store information that you choose to share with them.

Data Security

BuddyGo takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal data.

We implement security practices designed to safeguard personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures may include secure servers, encryption technologies, access control systems, and regular monitoring of our infrastructure.

Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel and service providers who require such access in order to operate, maintain, or improve the BuddyGo services.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, no online platform can guarantee absolute security. Therefore, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, hacking, data loss, or other security incidents will never occur.

You are also responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for protecting access to your device. If you believe that your account or personal data may have been compromised, please contact us immediately at:

support@buddygo.app

Your Privacy Rights

BuddyGo is committed to respecting your privacy and providing you with control over your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Depending on your country of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These rights may be subject to legal limitations, including the protection of other individuals’ rights, legal obligations, or security requirements.

Your privacy rights may include the following:

Right of Access

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to obtain a copy of that information in a commonly used format.

Right to Rectification

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal information. In many cases, you can update your information directly within your BuddyGo account settings.

Right to Erasure

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data (also known as the “right to be forgotten”). You may also delete your BuddyGo account directly through the account settings in the application.

Right to Withdraw Consent

If we process your personal data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. This can usually be done through the application settings or your device settings.

Right to Object to Processing

You may object to the processing of your personal data in certain situations where the processing is based on our legitimate interests.

Right to Restrict Processing

In certain cases, you may request the restriction of the processing of your personal data, for example if you contest the accuracy of the data or if the processing is unlawful.

Exercising Your Rights

You can exercise many of your privacy rights directly within the BuddyGo application through the account and privacy settings.

If you are unable to exercise your rights through the app, you may contact us at:

support@buddygo.app

We will review your request and respond within a reasonable time in accordance with applicable laws.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe that your personal data is being processed in violation of applicable laws, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

However, we encourage you to contact us first so that we can try to resolve the issue directly.

support@buddygo.app

Your continued use of the BuddyGo application or services after such updates indicates that you acknowledge and accept the revised Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your personal data.
