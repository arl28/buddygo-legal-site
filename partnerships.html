Partnerships

BuddyGo collaborates with selected travel and hospitality partners to enhance the experience of our users.

BuddyGo works with hotels, accommodations, and travel-related businesses to provide exclusive benefits to selected members of our community.

Through these partnerships, BuddyGo users may access travel-related perks, special offers, or discounts designed to make their travel experience more rewarding.

If you are a hotel, travel brand, hospitality group, or service provider interested in working with BuddyGo, we would love to hear from you.

For partnership inquiries, please contact: partners@buddygo.app
