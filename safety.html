Safety

Safety tips

Travel and meet people with confidence using these simple, effective recommendations.

In case of immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

Before meeting

  • Chat in the app before sharing social accounts.
  • Review profile details and photos.
  • Choose a public place and a clear time.

During the meeting

  • Tell a friend where you are and when you plan to return.
  • Stay aware of your surroundings.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information.

After the meeting

  • Report any inappropriate behavior.
  • Block the user if needed.
  • Share feedback to improve safety.

Data protection

  • Avoid sending identity documents.
  • Keep conversations inside the app.
  • Use a strong and unique password.

BuddyGo — Your safety is our priority.
