Safety Tips

Introduction

BuddyGo helps travelers connect and discover new destinations together. While meeting new people can lead to great experiences, your safety should always come first.

These safety tips are designed to help you use BuddyGo responsibly and reduce potential risks when interacting with other users online or in person.

Protect Your Personal Information

When using BuddyGo, avoid sharing sensitive personal information with people you have just met online.

You should never share details such as your home address, financial information, identification documents, or passwords. Be cautious when sharing your phone number, social media accounts, or other personal contact details.

Keeping your personal information private helps protect you from scams, fraud, or unwanted contact.

Verify Profiles Carefully

While BuddyGo encourages authentic profiles, it is important to remain cautious when interacting with new people.

Take time to review profiles, photos, and conversations before building trust. If a user appears suspicious, inconsistent, or asks for money or personal information, you should stop the conversation and report the account.

Trust your instincts if something does not feel right.

Be Careful With Financial Requests

BuddyGo strictly prohibits scams or requests for money between users.

If someone asks you for money, gifts, cryptocurrency, or financial assistance, this is a strong warning sign. Do not send money or financial information to people you only know through the app.

Always report users who attempt to request money or financial support.

Meeting in Person

BuddyGo allows travelers to connect, and some users may choose to meet in person. If you decide to meet someone you met through the app, always take precautions.

Meet in public places such as cafés, restaurants, or popular public areas. Inform a friend or family member about your plans and location. Arrange your own transportation and avoid relying on someone you just met for travel or accommodation.

Your safety should always be your top priority.

Stay Alert While Traveling

When traveling, it is important to stay aware of your surroundings and avoid risky situations.

Always keep your belongings secure and be cautious when sharing travel plans with people you have just met. Avoid isolated areas when meeting someone for the first time.

Using common sense and staying alert can significantly reduce potential risks.

Reporting Unsafe Behavior

BuddyGo provides reporting tools to help maintain a safe community.

If you encounter harassment, scams, suspicious behavior, or any activity that violates our Community Guidelines, please report it directly through the app.

Our moderation team reviews reports and takes appropriate action to protect the community.

Blocking Users

If another user makes you uncomfortable, you can block them at any time through the app. Blocking prevents that user from contacting you again.

Using the block and report features helps keep BuddyGo a safer place for everyone.

Emergency Situations

BuddyGo cannot intervene in real-world emergencies. If you ever feel that your safety is at risk or you encounter a dangerous situation, contact your local emergency services immediately.

Your personal safety is always more important than continuing a conversation or interaction online.

Our Commitment to Safety

BuddyGo is committed to creating a respectful and secure environment for travelers.

We continuously improve our moderation systems and community guidelines to help protect users and reduce harmful behavior on the platform.

By following these safety recommendations, you help create a safer experience for yourself and for the entire BuddyGo community.
